Paula’s Credentials

Professional Training

Completed advanced course training through Harvard Medical School:

  • Year long hypnotherapy externship
  • Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Core Conflictual Relationship Theme (CCRT)
  • Mentalization Based Therapy (MBT)
  • Year long externship in the Treatment of Attachment Disruption and Disorders in Adults

Achievements

  • Contributing author to the book “Attachment Disturbances in Adults,” recipient of the 2017 ISST-D Pierre Janet Writing Award.
  • In 2010, governor Deval Patrick appointed Paula Sacks to the Commonwealth of Massachusetts Board of Registration of Social Work where she retired in 2015 as Board Chairman.
  • From 2004-2008, Paula administered assessments and psychotherapy for low income clients, and in-house Attachment Theory training for staff and volunteer therapists at Community Legal Services and Counseling Center, Cambridge, Massachusetts.
